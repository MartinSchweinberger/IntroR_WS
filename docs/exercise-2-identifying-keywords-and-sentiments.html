Section 4 Exercise 2: Identifying Keywords and Sentiments

EXERCISE TIME


Have a look at the texts in the folder called ex2_sentiment (you can find it in the exercise folder in the data folder - you may need to unzip them before you can inspect them).
The aim is to determine which of the reviews are positive and which are negative and to identify words that indicate what group a review belongs to.
